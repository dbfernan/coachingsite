Local Coaching From a Seasoned Player

Hello, I'm David -- Tennis has been one of my biggest passions for many years, and by using my knowledge of the sport and high-level competitive experience, I want to play a part in the stories of future generations in CNY through coaching! As I'm now attending Syracuse University, my days of Junior-level USTA tournaments and Varsity are over; however, I'm still playing and competing in various clubs/ leagues. I've continued to improve my game, especially on a mental level, and as a coach I aim to teach what I know about form, tactics, mentality, etc, all while having fun and developing passion for Tennis in new players!

Playing/ Coaching Experiences

  • Played on FM's Varsity Team from 7th-12th Grade
  • #1 Singles Player from 11th-12th Grade (COVID-19 was year before)
  • Won many Junior-level USTA Tournaments
  • Played in several Regional-level and Sectional-level Championships for various leagues
  • Completed USTA's Coaching Workshop in Parsippany, NJ

Additionally, I've volunteered as a coach for Aceing Autism, worked as an assistant coach for Drumlins' Summer Tennis Camp, and worked as a coach for FM's Summer Tennis Camp in 2025!
